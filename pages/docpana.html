Academic Background

He is a holder of a Master of Technology Degree in Pharmacy from Harare Institute of Technologyy, India and a Bachelor of Technology (Honours) Degree in Anesthesiologists etc


Experience

Mr Makombe is the Managing Director/Chief Finance Officer of DeanDryn Hospital. He joined MIL from the First Mutual Group. He joined First Mutual Life Assurance Company (Private) Limited in June 2010 as the Finance Manager and rose to the position of Head of Finance for fellow group company First Mutual Health Company (Private) Limited, after successfully participating in the growth and demerger of the health division of the group. He held this position from September 2014 to the time he joined MIL. Mr. Makombe is a Anesthesiologists by profession. He has over six years post qualification experience after training conducted in India. He is also a holder of a Masters in Pharmacy.
